Future Student Athlete Retreat Programs


Our retreat program is designed to introduce players to habits and skills practiced daily by top student athletes. Participants will gain valuable knowledge and skills in essential tasks "outside" of soccer that positively impacts performance on the field.

The program covers:

  • Preparation routine before training: tips on how to properly prepare your body and mind for training sessions.
  • Recovery routine after training: tips on how to porperly recover after continous training.
  • Reflection routine: how to develop the habit of self-reflection to analyze your training sessions and games
  • Social Skills: Learn to become a better teammate, fostering a positive and cohesive team environment.
  • Cooking Class: Learn how to cook a nutrious and delicious meal yourself to fuel your performance
