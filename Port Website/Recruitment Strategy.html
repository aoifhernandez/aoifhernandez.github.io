College Recruitment Strategy

Recruitment Communication Strategy

Move will provide players with step by step guide to navigate a college recruiting process.

For many athletes, college recruiting process can be very confusing and time-consuming. Move is here to teach the process of recruting process in depth and help athletes learn how to initiate the recruitment process step by step. Why is this an important concept and skill to learn? College coaches are usually overwhelmed by the pool of athletes in their recruiting process. If athletes meet the basic requirements to be recruited (such as school grades, soccer skill set, and good character), showing strong interest and taking the initiative in the recruitment process will allow atheltes to stand out against other recruits!

Recruitment Initiation Strategy
