About Us

Reach Your Full Potential


    At MOVE, we specialize in empowering soccer athletes as they navigate the transition to college sports through the following services. (1) Personalized Training Sessions: We provide position-specific and personalized training programs for each player to elevate their performance to the next level. (2) College Recuirtment Support: We ensure our athletes are informed with the tools and guidance needed to play at the collegiate level. (3) Soccer Athlete Retreat Programs: Through our retreat programs, athletes will learn how to build better habits to help themselves succeed in a competitive and demanding environment.

Not a soccer athlete? No Problem.
Let's Imporve Our Fitness Together

In addition to our soccer athlete development program, we offer community-oriented fitness coaching tailored to each individual's goals and aspirations. Whether you're a retired athlete trying to get back in shape or full-time worker with limited time, we will help you unlock your full potential and achieve lasting results. At Move, we believe in the power of community. We provide personalized training program, lifestyle coaching, and community support you need to achieve your fitness goals. Join us and experience the strength of moving forward together.

About Me

After finishing my collegiate career at San Jose State University, I decided to build a platform to support the next generation of athletes. Throughout my 20-year career, I have gained diverse experience playing for the Okinawa Japan Regional Selection Team, Youth Academy Teams (U-16/18 De Anza Force Academy), California State University of Monterey Bay MSOC (NCAA DIV 2), and San Jose State University MSOC (NCAA DIV 1). Overcoming academic, cultural, and athletic obstacles while playing in both countries pushed me to refine every aspect of my game. I look forward to improving athletes' performance by enhancing their skills, game IQ, training habits, and mindset to become top performers of their game.
